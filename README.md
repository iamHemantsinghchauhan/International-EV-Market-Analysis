# 🚗 International EV Market Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Executive Summary

The global Electric Vehicle (EV) market is undergoing rapid transformation, driven by sustainability goals and technological advancements.
This project analyzes **150,482+ EV records** to uncover trends in adoption, manufacturer dominance, and regional growth patterns using an interactive Tableau dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem 

Despite rapid EV adoption, businesses and policymakers face challenges in identifying:

* Which EV segment (Battery vs Plug-in Hybrid) is driving growth
* Which manufacturers are leading and where competition is intensifying
* Which regions present the highest opportunity for EV expansion
* How EV adoption trends are evolving over time

👉 Without clear insights, companies risk **misallocating resources, missing high-growth markets, and losing competitive advantage**.

---

## 💼 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Provide a **data-driven view of the EV ecosystem**
* Enable stakeholders to identify **growth opportunities and market leaders**
* Support **strategic decision-making** using visual analytics

## 🔍 Analytical Approach

* Trend analysis of EV adoption over time
* Segmentation of EV types (BEV vs PHEV)
* Manufacturer-wise performance comparison
* Regional distribution using geospatial mapping
* KPI analysis (Total Vehicles, Avg Range)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📊 EV adoption has accelerated significantly post-2020
* 🚘 **Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)** dominate market share
* 🏆 **Tesla** leads the EV market with a strong margin
* 🌍 High adoption is concentrated in specific regions, indicating targeted growth opportunities
* 🔋 Average vehicle range is steadily improving, reflecting technological advancements
